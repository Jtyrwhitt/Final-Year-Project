Mathematical Operators

Python provides several arithmetic operators to perform mathematical operations.

Operator Name Description Example Output
+ Addition Adds two numbers 5 + 3 8
- Subtraction Subtracts the second number from the first 10 - 4 6
* Multiplication Multiplies two numbers 6 * 2 12
/ Division Divides first number by second (returns float) 9 / 2 4.5
// Floor Division Divides and rounds down to nearest whole number 9 // 2 4
% Modulus Returns remainder of a division 10 % 3 1
** Exponentiation Raises first number to the power of second 2 ** 3 8

Examples:

Addition (+)

Adds two numbers together.

Output: 10

Subtraction (-)

Subtracts the right-hand number from the left.

Output: 6

Multiplication (*)

Multiplies two numbers.

Output: 12

Division (/)

Divides and returns a float.

Output: 4.5

Floor Division (//)

Divides and rounds down to the nearest whole number.

Output: 4

Modulus (%)

Returns the remainder of a division.

Output: 1

Exponentiation (**)

Raises a number to the power of another.

Output: 8
